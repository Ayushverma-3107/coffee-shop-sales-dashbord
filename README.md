# ☕ Coffee Shop Sales Dashboard

An interactive Excel dashboard built to analyze coffee shop sales performance, customer footfall, product trends, and store-level insights. This dashboard provides a comprehensive overview of sales data using Pivot Tables, Pivot Charts, KPIs, and Slicers for dynamic filtering.

## 📊 Dashboard Overview

The dashboard presents key business metrics and visual insights, including:

### Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
- **Total Sales:** $698,812.33
- **Total Footfall:** 149,116
- **Average Bill per Person:** 4.69
- **Average Orders per Person:** 1.44

## 📈 Visualizations Included

### 1. Quantity Ordered Based on Hours
- Displays customer ordering patterns throughout the day.
- Helps identify peak business hours.

### 2. Category-wise Sales Distribution
- Pie chart showing sales contribution from different product categories:
  - Coffee
  - Tea
  - Bakery
  - Flavours
  - Packaged Chocolate
  - Drinking Chocolate
  - Coffee Beans
  - Loose Tea

### 3. Size Distribution Based on Orders
- Shows percentage of orders by cup size:
  - Large
  - Regular
  - Small
  - Not Defined

### 4. Footfall and Sales by Store Location
- Compares customer traffic and sales across:
  - Astoria
  - Hell's Kitchen
  - Lower Manhattan

### 5. Top 5 Products Based on Sales
- Highlights the highest revenue-generating products.
- Useful for inventory and marketing decisions.

### 6. Orders by Day of Week
- Analyzes ordering trends across weekdays and weekends.
